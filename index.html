About me

I am Maribel and I´mUX/UI Designer.

 I have dedicated myself to many things during these years, I studied in a television school, I was for a couple of years doing an internship as an Audiovisual Technician. I worked as a teleoperator, cashier, administrative in logistics ... In my last job I was in charge of managing the administrative management of large accounts of a food distributor. I have done several courses in design, video editing and postproduction, although I also like to play and learn for myself. During the last years I have felt stuck in a job that I did not like (although I always did it in the best way possible), for that reason I decided to change course and start to learn something that really likes me, that´s why I am in the world of UX / UI.
